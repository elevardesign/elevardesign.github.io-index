Our Process

For the average ecommerce company, roughly 98% of visitors leave without ever making a purchase. Our process aims to shrink that number and turn visitors into customers.

1. Research

We start by taking a deep dive into your data and analytics to identify gaps in the purchase funnel. We pair that analysis with industry trends, best practices and our UX expertise to form actionable hypotheses

2. Design

In the design phase, we translate the ideas and analyses into data-driven designs. We design within your companyâ€™s branding and design system to ensure a cohesive user experience.

3. Development

Once designs are finished and approved, we begin development. We have experience working with various ecommerce platforms and turn our designs in to working tests ready for deployment.

4. Implement

Using the Google platform, we implement the A/B test. We distribute traffic evenly across both variants to isolate the proposed change and effectively measure the difference in revenue per visitor (RPV). Our tests run a minimum of 2 weeks with a 95% confidence threshold.

Straight-forward Pricing

We offer both performance-based packages and monthly packages. With performance-based pricing, you only pay for actual growth. We measure success by revenue per visitor, which takes into account conversion rate and average order value. If our hypothesis is proven to be true, you pay 75% of the lift over the course of the test. Once the test ends, you keep 100% of the uplift in revenue. Our monthly fees start at 1% of monthly sales up to $1,000.
